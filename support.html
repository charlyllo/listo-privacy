Before You Contact Us

To help us assist you better, please include the following information in your email:

  • Device type: iPhone, Android, or other
  • Operating system version: iOS 17.x, Android 14, etc.
  • App version: Check in your device's app store
  • Description of the issue: What happened and what you expected
  • Steps to reproduce: If it's a bug, how can we recreate it?

Response Time

We typically respond to support emails within 24-48 hours. For urgent issues, please mark your email as "URGENT" in the subject line.

Privacy & Support

When you contact us for support:

  • We may ask for device information to help diagnose issues
  • We will never ask for your personal task data
  • All support communications are kept confidential
  • We don't store your support emails longer than necessary
